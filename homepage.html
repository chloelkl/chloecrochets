10 July, 2023

< >

I decided to crochet this cute chubby little cat as a birthday gift for my friend. Obviously I had to name her purpur because it's purple.

Making this was tougher than I'd expected. Deciding the placements of the eyes, embroidery, and body parts was hard because the cat is round and the stuffing may get in the way. My tip for this is to use A LOT of pins to have a rough idea of what goes where, and adjust before sewing everything in. I managed to pull through and she turned out adorable.
